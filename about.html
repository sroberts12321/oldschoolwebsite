what is vaporwave?

Vaporwave is a movement of music described as an ironic variant of 'chillwave'. Stylistically it is heavily influenced by 80's and 90's mood music, pulling from smooth jazz, elevator music, and lounge music. It commonly involves the sampling or manipulation of other tracks. The surrounding subculture is sometimes associated with an ambiguous or satirical take on consumer capitalism and popular culture. The aesthetic of the music genre often involves a mix of nostalgic or surrealist imagery, and incorporates glitch art, anime, 3D rendered objects, and backgrounds with dystopian cyperpunk themes.
